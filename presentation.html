üí° Project Idea - Overview

The Problem

Traditional restaurant ordering systems face multiple challenges:

  • Manual order taking leads to errors and miscommunication
  • Customers wait long times to place orders
  • Kitchen staff struggle with paper orders
  • No real-time tracking for delivery orders
  • Difficulty managing peak hours efficiently

Our Solution

A comprehensive Restaurant Ordering System that digitizes and automates the entire ordering workflow from customer to kitchen to delivery.
